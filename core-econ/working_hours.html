Description
  • Comparing development of average annual wages to average annual hours worked per worker.

Data source
OECD.Stat: "Average annual hours actually worked per worker" (code ANHRS, total employment only) and "Average annual wages" (code AV_AN_WAGE). Average hourly wages are computed by dividing AV_AN_WAGE with ANHRS.
